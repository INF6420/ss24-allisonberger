Allison Berger


I began taking classes in the MLIS program at Wayne State University in Fall 2022. During my time in the program, I have been working on earning my MLIS degree, along with a Graduate Certificate in Public Library Services to Children and Young Adults. I plan to graduate in December 2024. I am currently working as a Circulation Specialist at a suburban public library. After graduation, I am hoping to work as a youth services librarian at a suburban public library.


Before pursuing my master's degree, I earned my undergraduate degree at Wayne State University in December 2020. I graduated with a BA in Public Relations and a minor in Spanish. Following graduation, I worked as a Communications Assistant for almost 3 years.


Outside of work and school, I spend most of my time with my family, my friends, and my Great Dane Maximus. I also enjoy reading, watching anime, traveling, and playing soccer.
