Allison Berger


Allison Berger began in the MLIS program at Wayne State in Fall 2022. During her time in the program, she has been working on earning her MLIS degree, along with a Graduate Certificate in Public Library Services to Children and Young Adults. She plans to graduate in December 2024. She currently works as a Circulation Specialist at a suburban public library. After graduation, she is hoping to work as a youth services librarian at a suburban public library.


Before pursuing her master's degree, she earned her undergraduate degree at Wayne State University in December 2020. She graduated with a BA in Public Relations and a minor in Spanish. Following graduation, she worked as a Communications Assistant for almost 3 years.


Outside of work and school, Allison spends most of her time with her family, friends, and her Great Dane Maximus. She also enjoys reading, watching anime, traveling, and playing soccer.
